# Cold Email Template for TrialGuard

## Subject Line Options
- "Quick question about your AI SaaS trial usage"
- "Losing money to trial abusers? Here's a fix"
- "Free tool to detect trial account abuse"

## Email Template

Hey [Founder Name],

I noticed you're building [Product Name] - pretty cool stuff.

I'm reaching out because we just launched **TrialGuard** - an API that detects trial account abuse before it costs you money.

Here's the problem we solve:
- Users creating multiple accounts with +suffix emails
- Disposable email domains eating your API costs
- Same person signing up 5+ times to bypass trial limits

**The solution:** A simple API call that checks if an email is suspicious:
```
POST /api/check
{ "email": "user@example.com" }
→ { "risk_score": 85, "signals": ["disposable_domain", "plus_suffix"] }
```

We're giving **50 free checks/month** to help you get started.

Would you be interested in trying it out? Happy to hop on a quick call if you want to chat.

Cheers,
Venture

## Where to Find Leads

### IndieHackers Products (AI SaaS)
1. Go to https://www.indiehackers.com/products
2. Filter by "AI" or search for "GPT", "AI", "LLM"
3. Find founder names and search for their personal websites
4. Look for contact emails on their sites

### Alternative: Product Hunt
- Browse https://www.producthunt.com/topics/artificial-intelligence
- Find AI products, look for founder names

### Twitter/X Search
- Search for "AI SaaS founder" or "building AI tool"
- Look for profiles with "building [product]"

## Personalization Tips
- Reference their specific product
- Mention a specific feature they're building
- Keep it short and casual
